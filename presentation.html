HELLO, I'M A WEB & GRAPHIC DESIGNER

Hi, I'm Francesco and I'm a web and graphic designer. First of all, I would like to thank you for visiting my website, I hope you'll find it interesting. I start telling you something of my life related to design and to the passion that I achieved over the years: in particoular I like to design, to develop and to understand the functionality of things in general since ever. After this preamble, I try to resume the road to "how I became a designer ". I have been a curious kid and I really enjoyed my time drawing on every surface. I remember my father used to draw for me a football player kicking a penalty kick(and scoring as well) and I dreamt of being that guy(cause my favourite sport was football at the time). I was enchanted to see him drawing, and I have been repeating to myself that I would become a creative in life. Growing up, my desire evolved into a passion and I started drawing everyday, making portraits, copying things as environment, houses, streets, tools. During the high school this passion has been set aside for a while but it came back later stronger than ever. This was the reason why I signed up to Comics International School(illustration & visual arts school) in Pescara.

After the graduation I also took another important decision to concretize my education. I tryed to subscribe myself to a master in Milan(OPPI MiMaster), a master of illustration and graphic design. It's been a beautiful experience, where I learnt how best italian publishing houses and magazines use to work. So,this journey took me to become a professional illustrator but not a complete designer yet; i thought I had to increase my preparation to work as a designer. So, I started to attend a graphic design course during lockdown. The school is named JOB FORMAZIONE and helped me to understand graphic design and UI/UX design rules. After all of these experiences, I found my first job as a designer in a serigraphy. I still work there after more than 2 years. But it wasn't enough again(LOL). My goal in life is to grow as human being in every aspect. I have heard about Start2Impact, a beautiful new reality in digital design and I started a web developing course.
